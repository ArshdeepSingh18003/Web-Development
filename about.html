BOOKS GALLERY

Books gallery is all about different types of books that user can read in their free time. Their are vast amounts of books to choose from and every book placed in their own category. You can read any book summary with no cost.

Books Gallery begans in 2018 as a school project by IMDW student Arshdeep-Singh. Their teacher give them a project to make a website. And from that day coding of this website started. Main motive of this website is to spread knowledge with no cost. By just reading 5 minute summary you can get to know about a whole book, and your knowledge will also increase. The main line of Books gallery is "Smarter you in 5-minutes".
